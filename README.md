## The tiers

**⚡ THE ELEMENTALS (8)** — the outer powers and primal forces, each lit in its own element:
the **Greater Will** (gold Order) · the **Frenzied Flame** (yellow chaos) · the **Scarlet Rot** (the bloom that devours) · **Destined Death** (the Rune removed from the Ring) · the **Formless Mother** (blood) · the **Fell God's Flame** (the fire that burns the Erdtree) · the **Ancient Dragons' Red Lightning** (the storm before the gold) · the **Crucible** (the primordial blend of all life).

**👑 THE ETERNALS (6)** — those who stand outside death and time, sealed in gold:
**Queen Marika the Eternal** · **Dragonlord Placidusax** (waiting outside time) · the **Eternal Cities** (Nokron & Nokstella) · **Miquella** (the curse of eternal youth) · **Ranni the Witch** (the Age of Stars) · the **Elden Beast** (the Ring made flesh).

**The cast & the frame (15)** — the Tarnished, Melina, Torrent; Godfrey, Morgott, Mohg, Radahn, Malenia, Rykard, Godrick, Rennala, Maliketh; the Elden Ring, the Erdtree, grace.

## The four natures

- **natural** — the embodied: the warriors and beasts, the rot and the fire, the land itself
- **ethereal** — the unseen: the Outer Gods, fate, the night and the moon
- **spiritual** — the golden: godhood, grace, faith, and sacrifice
- **electrical** — the storm: the **red lightning of the ancient dragons**, the oldest power still wielded

## A fan tribute — and the lore is a riddle

Elden Ring is the creation of **FromSoftware** (dir. Hidetaka Miyazaki, worldbuilding with George R. R. Martin), © FromSoftware / Bandai Namco. This is an **unofficial homage** — original commentary and badge-work, no game text or assets reproduced. Elden Ring's lore is **deliberately ambiguous**; what is sealed here is a *reading*, hedged where the game hedges — and "the Elementals" / "the Eternals" are this catalogue's own organizing frame.
